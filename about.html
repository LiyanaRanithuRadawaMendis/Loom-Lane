Our Story

Loom & Lane was born from a passion for bringing the timeless beauty of Sri Lankan craftsmanship to modern homes. We believe that every piece of decor should tell a story—of culture, heritage, and the skilled hands that made it.

What started as a small curated collection has grown into a destination for those seeking unique, sustainable, and soulful additions to their living spaces. From handwoven textiles to intricate wood carvings, we bridge the gap between traditional artisans and contemporary design lovers.

Our Mission

We are driven by three core values that shape everything we do.

Preserving Heritage

Keeping ancient techniques alive by supporting local artisans and their communities.

Sustainable Living

Using eco-friendly materials like organic cotton, clay, and reclaimed wood.

Timeless Design

Creating pieces that transcend trends and become cherished heirlooms in your home.

Connect With Us

Follow our journey, get inspired by our latest collections, and join our community of design lovers.
